Birria Tacos Recipe

Bangin' Birria Tacos

You don't want to miss out on learning how to make these amazing, juicy, crispy birria tacos served with a side of consommé!

If you like flavorful short-rib meat paired with cheesy goodness and with a little bit of spice, you will LOVE how great this dish tastes.

Ingredients:

  • short ribs (bone-in)
  • bay leaves
  • oregano, cumin, salt and chili powder
  • white onions
  • quesadilla cheese
  • corn tortillas
  • guajillo peppers

Steps:

  1. Add all ingredients to a 7 qt dutch oven.
  2. Cover with water and simmer.
  3. After 30 minutes of simmering, skim the top of the broth from all impurities.
  4. Remove the guajillo peppers and blend in a food processor or blender and put back into the broth.
  5. Continue to simmer for approx. 3 hours until meat is tender, stirring about every 40 minutes.
  6. Take grease that was skimmed from the top and add to a skillet.
  7. Take corn flour tortillas and fry them in the grease over medium heat.
  8. Add the quesadilla cheese and shredded birria beef on top and fold over.
  9. ENJOY! Your taste buds will thank you.
