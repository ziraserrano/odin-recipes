Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe

Oh-So-Spicy Buffalo Chicken Dip

Your first bite of this mouth-watering dish will have your taste buds explode!

With delicious cheeses and Frank's Red Hot Sauce, you'll want this as an meal for breakfast, lunch, and dinner!

Ingredients:

  • cooked shredded chicken
  • Frank's Original Red Hot Sauce
  • cream cheese
  • cheddar cheese
  • american cheddar cheese
  • crumbled blue cheese (optional)
  • green onions sliced (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Add all ingredients to 8-inch cast-iron skillet.
  3. Mix until sauce thickens.
  4. Reduce heat to low and add cheddar cheese on top.
  5. Bake until cheese has melted.
  6. Broil for another minute or until brown on top.
  7. Garnish with green onions and blue cheese crumbles and serve immediately.
