Lasagna Recipe

The Best Lasagna!

It's not called the best lasagna for nothing! This dish is packed with gooey cheese, delicious meat and flavorful sauce (or gravy if that's your thing).

If you aren't making THIS lasagna for your family, then what are you doing!? Follow the recipe below for the best lasagna!

Ingredients:

    meat sauce white sauce parmesan cheese lasagna sheets

Steps:

    Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Make the meat sauce. Make the white sauce. Spoon the meat sauce at the base of the dish. Cover with lasagna sheets. Spoon the meat sauce over the lasagna sheets. Spoon the white sauce over the meat sauce. Repeat steps 5-7 as desired. Pour the remaining meat sauce and white sauce over the last layer of lasagna sheets and top with parmesan cheese. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden and bubbly. Enjoy the best lasagna!
